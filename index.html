About Me

Hi! I'm a student at Grinnell College studying Mathematics and Linguistics. I have a particular passion for the mathematics of language, formal programming, and Linux. I love appreciating the thought behind our everyday technology.

Currently, I spend my time as an undergraduate student and researcher, repairing my old sewing machine, and (trying) to make crosswords. For those who are curious, I think Emacs is the better text editor. ;-)

My Research

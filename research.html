Homotopical Combinatorics

Summer 2025

Researched under Professor Christy Hazel on enumeration of "transfer systems," a combinatorial object relevant in G-equivariant homotopy theory. I produced a novel recurrence for a certain class of transfer systems; a poster is prepared for presentation at the 2026 AMS Joint Math Meeting, and a paper is scheduled to be prepared in Fall 2025. The paper will be uploaded here when it's in preprint.

I offer special thanks to Professor Hazel for her mentorship in this project!

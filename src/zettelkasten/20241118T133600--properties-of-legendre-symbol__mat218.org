* Properties of Legendre Symbol

1. Multiplicativity
   \[(a/p)(b/p) \rightarrow (ab/p)\]
2. Euler's Criterion
   \[(a/p)=a^{(p-1)/2}\]
3. Modular Equivalence
   \[a\equiv b \pmod{p} \rightarrow (a/p)=(b/p)\]
4. Quadratic Reciprocity
   \[(p/q)(q/p) = (-1)^{\frac{p-1}{2}\cdot\frac{q-1}{2}}\]
5. $a = -1$
   \[(-1/p) = (-1)^{\frac{p-1}{2}}\]
6. $a = 2$
   \[(2/p) = (-1)^{\frac{p^2-1}{2}}\]
7. $a = x^2$
   \[p\nmid x \leftrightarrow (x^2/p) = 1\]
